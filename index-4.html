How to take care of your child's health?

Every child needs proper care, timely nutrition, and regular checkups for healthy growth. Parents should focus on simple daily habits that keep the child active, safe, and free from common illnesses. With small changes in routine, children can grow strong and develop better.

Follow the instructions

  • Give your child a balanced and nutritious diet every day.
  • Keep vaccinations up to date as advised by the doctor.
  • Encourage outdoor play and limit excess screen time.
  • Maintain regular health checkups for growth monitoring.
