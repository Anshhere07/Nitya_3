Experience of Dr. Shweta Mishra

Dr. Shweta Mishra has been serving as a Pathology Specialist since 2016 at Satyanand Hospital, Uttar Pradesh, where she provides expert diagnostic support for patients across various medical conditions. With her strong foundation in pathology and dedication to precision, she ensures that every test and report contributes to better treatment outcomes.

With more than 9 years of clinical experience, Dr. Mishra combines her M.B.B.S. and M.D. qualifications to deliver timely and reliable diagnostic services. Her role as a pathology expert extends beyond just testing—she emphasizes clear interpretation and collaboration with physicians, ensuring patients receive the right care at the right time.

Qualifications of Dr. Shweta Mishra

Dr. Shweta Mishra began her medical journey with an M.B.B.S. degree from KML Manipal (2005–2010), where she built a strong foundation in medicine and patient care. Her early academic years were marked by dedication, discipline, and a passion for understanding human health. This commitment to excellence laid the groundwork for her specialization and future contributions to the medical field.

To further refine her expertise, she pursued an M.D. in Biochemistry at GMC Patiala (2013–2016), gaining in-depth knowledge of the biochemical aspects of health and disease. Throughout her academic path, she was recognized for her intellectual curiosity and consistent performance. Beyond her formal education, Dr. Mishra remains committed to continual learning, staying updated with the latest medical advancements to provide patients with the most accurate and effective diagnostic care.
